Editorial

Since the last issue of this newsletter, back in July 2008, Lokad has evolved a lot. We have been steadily improving our forecasting technology. Our Tags+Events framework lets us now handle complex market behaviors such as promotions and product launches. Then, about 6 months ago, we initiated our migration toward cloud computing in order to achieve unrivaled scalability.

We have been improving our client apps as well. Since the very beginning of Lokad, client apps, such as our sales forecasting add-in for Excel, have been the weakest link of our forecasting solution. Yet, I believe that, thanks to your feedback, we have been making steady progress in this area. If you've just had a look to Lokad a few months ago, I would suggest giving it another try.

Then, listening to and addressing questions from the community is extremely important for any company, but especially for a company such as Lokad since forecasting is in many ways a counter-intuitive process. That's why we have just launched a new platform called Ask Lokad where we will do our best to publicly address questions about forecasting and business optimization.

Best regards,
Joannes Vermorel, Founder at Lokad.


Ask Lokad and get answers

ask.lokad.com is a new social website to address questions about forecasting for business.

At Lokad, we realize, probably more than any other company, that forecasting is tough, and that companies need their questions addressed. Yet, typical web forums are either very restrictive, or plagued with spam, irrelevant or simply outdated content. The old forums initially setup by Lokad were no exception to this rule.

In the post-mortem analysis of those old forums, we realized that the community itself is a much better judge than Lokad, or any of our competitors for that matter, to define which questions (resp. answer) should be considered as most important (most useful).

Based on those conclusions, we have just setup ask.lokad.com as a Questions & Answers board where each question, each answer can be voted up or down by other contributors.

  • Ask a question, get votes and get points.
  • Answer a question, get votes and get even more points.

As part of our launch initiative, we have decided to offer $250 of pre-paid Lokad services to the first 20 users who will reach 1000 points.

Hint: each time your question or answer gets up-voted by someone, you get 10 points.

Got a question about forecasting in mind? Ask now and get your reward. To claim your reward, just drop an email to sales@lokad.com with ASK LOKAD in the message title.

Does your forecasting tool support tags and events?

A couple months ago, we released a major upgrade on our forecasting framework codenamed Tags+Events. Long story short, forecasting is tough, but if it becomes possible to tell the system about all similarities that exist between products, or about events that will most certainly impact the business, then the system can deliver much better forecasts.

This is exactly what our Tags+Events framework is about: refining forecasts by considering more information than the strict historical data of your company.

Then, Lokad does not ask you to express the impact of a promotion - computing the impact of a promotion is our job - but just to be informed (through tags+events) that a promotion will happen.
