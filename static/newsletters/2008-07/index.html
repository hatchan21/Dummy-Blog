Lokad Newsletter - July 2008

4x forecasts - price cut

Lokad has a pay-per-forecast pricing. Since launch, 18 months ago, this pricing stayed virtually unchanged. Yet, processing power has never been so cheap, and we are committed to provide top-notch services at a price that is affordable even for small companies.

Thus, we have decided to perform a major price cut, roughly dividing our prices by 4 for daily, weekly and monthly forecasts.

Updated typical pricing scenarios include:
  • Free up to 200 monthly forecasts (*)
  • $38 / month for 1000 monthly forecasts
  • $199 / month for 1500 weekly forecasts
  • $499 / month for 6000 weekly forecasts

(*) If applying for the partner plan.

Safety stock optimization

Lokad Safety Stock Calculator v1 (LSSC) is a new free add-on application dedicated to inventory management. Make the most of our sales forecasts through LSSC.

Key features:

  • Reorder points - optimize your replenishment orders.
  • Stock covers - how much time is left before the shortage.
  • Sort your report by suppliers.

LSSC already supports +20 third-party applications. Check it out.
